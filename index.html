Documents Required

Visa Requirements

1

Original Passport

Your current valid original passport. Must be submitted at the time of application processing.

2

Old Passport

Your previous/expired passport. Required to verify travel history and previous visa records.

3

Passport Size Photo

Recent passport-size photograph with white background. Should be clear, without glasses or head covering.

4

CNIC Copy

Clear copy of your valid Computerized National Identity Card (CNIC). Both front and back sides required.

5

Police Character Certificate

Police character certificate covering from since birth to today — complete lifelong record.

Apostille attestation from MOFA (Ministry of Foreign Affairs) is required on this certificate.

6

Bank Statement

Personal account bank statement of last 6 months with closing balance clearly mentioned.

Minimum closing balance: 1 Million PKR
7

Family Details for Application Form

Complete family details are required to fill in the China visa application form:

Father's full name
Mother's full name
Spouse name (if married)
Children names & DOB
Your date of birth
Place of birth
Nationality
Your occupation

Need Help with Documents?

Don't worry if any document seems complicated — especially the Police Character Certificate with MOFA apostille. We'll guide you step by step.
