Documents Required

Visa Requirements

1

Original Passport

Your current valid original passport. Must be submitted at the time of application processing.

2

Old Passport

Your previous/expired passport. Required to verify travel history and previous visa records.

3

Passport Size Photo

Recent passport-size photograph with white background. Clear, without glasses.

4

CNIC Copy

Clear copy of your valid CNIC. Both front and back sides required.

5

Police Character Certificate

Certificate covering from since birth to today.

Apostille attestation from MOFA is required on this certificate.

6

Bank Statement

Personal account statement of last 6 months.

Min balance: 1 Million PKR
7

Family Details for Application Form

Complete family details required for the China visa application form:

Father's name
Mother's name
Spouse name
Children & DOB
Your DOB
Place of birth
Nationality
